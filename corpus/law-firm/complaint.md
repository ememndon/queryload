GLOBEX DISTRIBUTION INC., Defendant.

Case No. CV-2023-04817
COMPLAINT FOR BREACH OF CONTRACT

1. Plaintiff Acme Provisions LLC ("Acme") brings this action against Defendant Globex Distribution Inc. ("Globex") for breach of the Master Supply Agreement dated 12 January 2023 (the "Agreement").

2. Under the Agreement, Globex agreed to deliver 40,000 crates of organic apples to Acme's Marlowe distribution center in three tranches, with final delivery due no later than 31 March 2023.

3. Globex delivered only the first tranche of 12,000 crates on 5 March 2023. No further deliveries were made. Globex has not cured the shortfall despite written notice.

4. As a direct result, Acme was unable to fulfill its own downstream commitments and incurred cover costs of $418,000 to procure replacement inventory at market rates.

5. Section 9.2 of the Agreement provides that the breaching party shall be liable for the non-breaching party's reasonable cover costs and consequential damages arising from a material breach.

WHEREFORE, Acme prays for judgment against Globex for damages of not less than $418,000, pre- and post-judgment interest, and costs of suit.
